Hello:

I'm Brian Ngan!

I'm a product manager and UX designer with four years experience in solving product problems at scale and designing great user experiences.

Currently, I am a part of the Carro product team, solving problems in the automotive fintech and car marketplace space. As the sole product manager for financial services, I drive the dealership financing system and transactions roadmap. In addition, I lead the designs on modules such as the auctions platform, notifications, e-signing and more..

Prior to this, I was the founding product manager of Photonico at Singapore Press Holdings. I brought the product to market and then grew the community to hundreds of photo contributors and thousands of monthly users. It was nominated on Awwwards and awarded special kudos on CSS Design Awards.

I was also a UX designer at Singapore Press Holdings and did UX research and usability tests on keystone products such as The Straits Times premium signaling, MySPH SSO, eshop and more..
